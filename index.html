AboutUs

Nike, a globally renowned sportswear and athletic company, has a rich history that dates back to 1964. It was founded as Blue Ribbon Sports by University of Oregon track athlete Phil Knight and his coach Bill Bowerman. Originally a distributor for the Japanese shoemaker Onitsuka Tiger, the company officially became Nike, Inc. in 1971. The iconic "swoosh" logo was designed for $35 by graphic design student Carolyn Davidson in 1971. The name "Nike" is inspired by the Greek winged goddess of victory, reflecting the brand's emphasis on triumph and success. Nike revolutionized the athletic footwear industry with its innovative designs and technologies. In 1979, the company introduced the Tailwind, featuring a cushioning system using gas-filled plastic membranes. The Air Max line, introduced in 1987, showcased visible air pockets in the shoe's sole, setting a new standard for comfort and style. One of Nike's landmark moments was the partnership with basketball legend Michael Jordan in 1984, giving rise to the immensely popular Air Jordan line. This collaboration not only transformed the athletic shoe market but also elevated the concept of athlete endorsements.
